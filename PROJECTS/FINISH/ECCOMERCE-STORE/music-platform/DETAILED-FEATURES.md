## Custom Ad System

### Overview
Build a comprehensive advertising platform similar to Google AdSense, AdMob, and Unity Ads that serves ads across the music platform and potentially to third-party apps.

### Ad System Architecture

**Components:**
1. Ad Server
2. Ad Exchange
3. Advertiser Dashboard
4. Publisher Dashboard (for third-party integration)
5. Ad Analytics Engine
6. Bidding System (RTB - Real-Time Bidding)
7. Ad Quality Control
8. Fraud Detection System

### Ad Formats

#### 1. Audio Ads
**Specifications:**
- Duration: 15s, 30s, 60s
- Format: MP3, AAC
- Bitrate: 128kbps minimum
- Sample rate: 44.1kHz
- Mono or Stereo

**Placement:**
- Between tracks (every 3-4 tracks for free users)
- Start of playlist
- Start of podcast episode
- During live streams (for free streamers)

**Features:**
- Skip after 5 seconds (optional)
- Companion banner (visual element)
- Click-through URL
- Call-to-action button
- Frequency capping
- Dayparting (time-based targeting)

#### 2. Video Ads
**Specifications:**
- Duration: 15s, 30s, 60s
- Format: MP4, WebM
- Resolution: 720p, 1080p
- Aspect ratio: 16:9, 9:16 (vertical), 1:1 (square)

**Placement:**
- Pre-roll (before content)
- Mid-roll (during long content)
- Post-roll (after content)
- In-feed (reels, social feed)
- Interstitial (between app sections)

**Types:**
- Skippable (after 5s)
- Non-skippable
- Rewarded (watch for premium feature)
- Interactive
- Shoppable

**Placement:**
- Top banner
- Bottom banner
- Sidebar
- In-feed
- Between content
- Profile pages
- Search results

**Types:**
- Static image
- Animated GIF
- HTML5 interactive
- Rich media

- Floating

#### 4. Native Ads
**Placement:**
- In music feed
- In playlist recommendations
- In search results

- Sponsored playlists
- Sponsored tracks

**Features:**
