# Bootstrap Guide: Starting Without Business Registration

## Overview

This guide helps you integrate third-party task providers when you don't have a registered business, website, privacy policy, or business email. We'll focus on providers that accept individual developers and provide workarounds for common barriers.

---

## Phase 4: Business Registration (Month 3-6)

### When to Register Your Business

**Register When:**

- Monthly revenue exceeds $1,000
- You want to access premium providers (Fyber, AdColony)
- You need to issue invoices
- You want business bank accounts

### Nigeria Business Registration Options

#### Option 1: Business Name Registration (Cheapest)

- **Cost**: ₦10,000 - ₦15,000
- **Time**: 2-4 weeks
- **Process**: Through CAC (Corporate Affairs Commission)
- **Good For**: Sole proprietorship

#### Option 2: Limited Liability Company (LLC)

- **Cost**: ₦50,000 - ₦100,000
- **Time**: 4-8 weeks
- **Process**: Through CAC
- **Good For**: Scaling, investor funding

#### Option 3: Use a Registration Service

- **Services**: LegalNaija, StartupNaija, BusinessNaija
- **Cost**: ₦30,000 - ₦150,000 (includes processing)
- **Time**: 2-3 weeks
- **Good For**: Hassle-free registration

## Provider Application Tips

### What to Say in Applications

**App Description Template:**

```
Earn9ja is a mobile task completion platform that connects Nigerian users
with earning opportunities. Users complete surveys, watch videos, and
engage with offers to earn money. We focus on providing legitimate,
accessible income opportunities for the Nigerian market.

Monthly Active Users: [Your current number or "Launching soon"]
Target Audience: Nigerian adults aged 18-45
Monetization: Commission on completed offers
```

**Why You're a Good Partner:**

- Focused on underserved Nigerian market
- High user engagement (mobile-first)
- Quality traffic (verified users)
- Growing user base

### Common Rejection Reasons & Solutions

**"Need a website"**
→ Use GitHub Pages (free, professional)

**"Need privacy policy"**
→ Use TermsFeed generator (free, compliant)

**"Need business email"**
→ Use Gmail with professional name or Zoho free plan

**"Need traffic proof"**
→ Start with providers that don't require it (CPAGrip, OGAds)
→ Come back after 1-2 months with real numbers

**"App not in store yet"**
→ Launch on Google Play first (easier approval)
→ Use TestFlight for iOS beta

## FAQ

**Q: Can I really start with just an email?**
A: Yes! CPAGrip, OGAds, and AdGate Media accept personal accounts.

**Q: Will providers accept a GitHub Pages website?**
A: Yes, as long as it has proper content (About, Privacy Policy, Contact).

**Q: Do I need a business bank account?**
A: No, most providers pay via PayPal, Payoneer, or Bitcoin to personal accounts.

**Q: What if I get rejected?**
A: Start with easier providers, build traffic, then reapply with real metrics.

**Q: How long until I need business registration?**
A: Only when you're making $1,000+/month or want premium providers.

**Q: Can I use a free domain (.tk, .ml)?**
A: Some providers accept them, but paid domains (.com) look more professional.
